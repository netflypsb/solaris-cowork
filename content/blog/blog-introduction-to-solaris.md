## The Problem with Modern Software

Today's knowledge workers juggle an average of 9-12 different SaaS tools daily. Project management in Asana, documentation in Notion, design in Figma, learning on Coursera, creative work in Canva, coding assistance in Copilot, and on and on.

Each tool is a silo. Each switch costs time. Each context gap costs accuracy. And your AI assistants? They only see what's inside their own walls.

The result: fragmentation, context switching, and AI that's smart in theory but disconnected in practice.

## The Solaris Solution

Solaris is a desktop AI application that combines everything into one unified, AI-powered workspace:

### AI Chat — Your Central Intelligence
At the core of Solaris is a powerful AI conversation system. But unlike standalone chatbots, Solaris AI has full access to:
- Your project files and directory structure
- Your configured tools and services (via MCP connectors)
- Over 170 specialized AI skills for domain expertise
- A sandboxed code execution environment
- Browser automation for web research and tasks

You don't just chat with Solaris — you **work** with it.

### Project Board — Built-in Project Management
A full Kanban board with:
- Five-column workflow: Backlog → To Do → In Progress → Review → Done
- Drag-and-drop task management
- Priority levels, labels, due dates, and subtasks
- Task linking to conversations and wiki pages
- List view for table-style task management

No more switching to Jira or Trello. Your tasks live where your work happens.

### Knowledge Base — Your Project Wiki
A wiki system with:
- Markdown editor with live preview
- Bidirectional wiki links (`[[Page Name]]`)
- Folder organization and tagging
- Templates for meeting notes, project briefs, and research
- Automatic backlink tracking

Every piece of knowledge is connected and searchable.

### Visual Editor — Design Without Leaving
A canvas-based visual editor with:
- Shape tools (rectangles, circles, text)
- Preset sizes for social media, print, and presentations
- Property controls for position, size, color, and opacity
- PNG export at full resolution
- Project gallery with thumbnails

Create diagrams, mockups, and graphics without opening Figma.

### Learning Hub — AI-Powered Education
An interactive learning system with:
- AI-generated learning experiences tailored to your project
- Quizzes, lessons, and skill challenges
- XP progression and streak tracking
- Multiple learner profiles
- Direct application of skills to your actual work

Learn exactly what you need, when you need it.

### Creative Hub — AI Content Generation
A gallery and management system for AI-generated content:
- Brochures, infographics, advertisements
- Algorithmic art and generative designs
- E-books, posters, and carousels
- Digital planners and presentations
- Automatic organization by type and date

Every piece of creative work the AI produces is saved and browsable.

## What Makes Solaris Different

### Unified Context
In Solaris, everything shares context. When you create a task on the Project Board, the AI knows about it during your next conversation. When you document a decision in the Wiki, it informs the AI's suggestions. Your learning progress influences creative recommendations.

This isn't just convenient — it's a **compound effect**. Each feature makes every other feature smarter.

### 170+ AI Skills
Solaris ships with an extensive library of AI skills:
- **Document skills**: PDF, XLSX, DOCX, PPTX creation and manipulation
- **Creative skills**: Advertisements, brochures, infographics, posters, e-books
- **Scientific skills**: 130+ specialized research and analysis skills
- **Browser automation**: Web research, form filling, data extraction
- **Custom skills**: Create your own domain-specific expertise

Skills are loaded on-demand, keeping the AI focused and efficient.

### MCP Connector Ecosystem
Through the Model Context Protocol, Solaris connects to an ever-growing ecosystem of external tools:
- Browser automation (Chrome)
- Database access
- GitHub, Notion, Slack integrations
- Custom API endpoints
- Community-built connectors

### Privacy First
- **Local-first storage**: All data stays on your machine in your project's `.solaris/` directory
- **Your API keys**: You use your own AI provider keys — no data passes through our servers
- **Sandboxed execution**: Code runs in isolated environments
- **No telemetry**: Your work is your work

### Bring Your Own Model
Solaris supports multiple AI providers:
- OpenRouter (access to 100+ models)
- Anthropic (Claude models)
- OpenAI (GPT models)
- xAI (Grok models)
- Custom endpoints (any compatible API)

Choose the model that works best for your use case, and switch anytime.
